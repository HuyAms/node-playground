## Good Reads

If you want to go deeper on logging and observability, these are worth your time:

- [Logging Sucks](https://loggingsucks.com/) — a blunt take on why traditional logging fails and what to do instead
- [Observability Wide Events 101](https://boristane.com/blog/observability-wide-events-101/) — intro to wide events as a superior alternative to scattered logs
- [Canonical Log Lines](https://stripe.com/blog/canonical-log-lines) — how Stripe structures a single rich log line per request for high-signal observability
